Welcome to the Robo Raptors Robotics Hub

The 2205H RoboRaptors VEX IQ team is proud to be part of Robospark Labs, a community of advanced teams focused on innovation and collaboration in robotics. This season, we won the League Teamwork Championship and qualified for the Washington State Championship, where we made it all the way to the Finals. Our journey reflects months of hard work, strategic design, and precise driving. From early scrimmages to high-stakes matches, we’ve grown as both engineers and teammates. We’re excited to keep improving, competing, and inspiring others along the way.
